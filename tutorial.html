One thing I have learnt and improved upon is my mindset. I used to procrastinate tasks such as coursework and studying because I always thought "why do it now when I can do it later and have fun now." This wasn't a good strategy because the work I had to do was always in the back of my mind which stressed me out and impacted by ability to enjoy things, it also meant that I rushed all my work right before the deadline so my work wouldn't be the best it could be. The first step to change was realising that this was a problem. I realised that the negatives far outweigh the positives and if I just did my work instead of putting it off I would be better off. This has made my mindset better because instead of thinking "Why do it now when I can do it later and have fun now" I open my work and try to do it. There are still things to work on, I find it hard to focus on my work when the deadline isn't right there but I still make a bit of progress which is better than nothing. In the future I can try and work on my focus.
